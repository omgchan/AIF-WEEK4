### FuseMachines AI Fellowship 2026

## Week 4: Linear Models

* Applied linear models to the Telecom Customer Churn dataset.
* Built classification models to predict customer churn (`Yes` / `No`) using customer behavior and service data.
* Learned different evaluation metrics such as Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-score, ROC-AUC, and PR-AUC, along with their importance in imbalanced datasets.
* Understood why accuracy alone can be misleading for churn prediction problems.
* Compared multiple linear classifiers including Logistic Regression, Ridge Classifier, and SGD Classifier.
* Explored threshold tuning and probability-based decision making for business constraints.
* Used regression models to predict customer tenure and estimate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
* Learned how regression outputs can provide meaningful business insights beyond binary churn prediction.
* Explored regularization techniques such as Ridge, Lasso, and Elastic Net and studied their effects on feature selection and model stability.
* Evaluated model generalization using cross-validation, learning curves, and residual analysis.
